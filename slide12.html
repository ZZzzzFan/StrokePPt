Conclusions & Acknowledgments

Main Conclusions

1

Bidirectional interactions exist among DP, DC, and CM in stroke couples

2

DC serves as a key mediator in DP‚ÜíCM pathways (both actor and partner effects)

3

Couple-centered interventions can improve CM and stroke recovery outcomes

Limitations

1. Cross-sectional design: No causal inference possible

2. Regional sample: Henan tertiary hospitals only

3. Moderating factors: Disease severity, economic status not examined
