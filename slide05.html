Theoretical Framework: APIMeM & Hypotheses

Actor-Partner Interdependent Mediation Model

Actor Effect (Self → Self) Partner Effect (Partner → Self)

Research Hypotheses

H1

Interdependent CM exists between patients and spouses

H2

DP predicts own/partner's CM (actor/partner effects)

H3

DC mediates DP→CM pathways

Key Concepts:
• Actor Effect: Individual's DP → own DC → own CM
• Partner Effect: Partner's DP → individual's DC → individual's CM
